The Future of Decentralized Subscriptions

FlowSub is your all-in-one Web3 subscription hub on Solana. Simplify recurring payments, stake our SUB token for rewards, and watch your favorite services integrate frictionless crypto billing.

How It Works

1. Connect your Solana wallet. 2. Deposit funds in SOL, USDC, or our SUB token. 3. Select the subscriptions you want. 4. FlowSub’s smart contract automates monthly payments, so you never miss a bill.

Powered by on-chain logic, FlowSub ensures transparent, tamper-proof transactions. No middlemen—just you and your favorite services, connected by Solana.

Why SUB Token?

SUB is our native token. When you use SUB for subscription payments, you receive discounts from participating providers. You can also stake SUB to reduce transaction fees and access premium features (like auto-renew for multiple addresses).

The more people adopt SUB, the more valuable it becomes—both as a payment method and an investment opportunity.

Invest Early

Early supporters who acquire SUB tokens now will benefit from lower prices and exclusive staking perks. As the user base grows, the demand for SUB increases, potentially raising its market value. That means cheaper subscription costs for early adopters, plus gains on your token holdings.
